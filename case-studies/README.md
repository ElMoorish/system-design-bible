# Case Studies

A collection of 12 real-world company architectures, analyzing how they solved scaling, reliability, and data challenges. These case studies serve as the "evidence" for the patterns discussed in the [Foundations](../README.md#foundations) and [AI Era](../README.md#ai-era) chapters.

### List of Case Studies

1. [Meta: TAO (The Social Graph)](./01-meta-tao.md) - Distributed graph caching at world-scale.
2. [Amazon: Dynamo](./02-amazon-dynamo.md) - The foundation of highly available NoSQL.
3. [Google: Spanner](./03-google-spanner.md) - Globally consistent distributed transactions.
4. [Netflix: Chaos Engineering](./04-netflix-chaos.md) - Building resilience through failure injection.
5. [Stripe: Payment Reliability](./05-stripe-payments.md) - Transactional outbox and idempotency.
6. [Uber: The Distributed Marketplace](./06-uber-marketplace.md) - Managing state with the Saga pattern.
7. [Airbnb: Search & Discovery](./07-airbnb-search.md) - Hybrid search and real-time availability.
8. [Discord: Trillions of Messages](./08-discord-messaging.md) - ScyllaDB and NVMe-optimized storage.
9. [Slack: Real-time Messaging](./09-slack-realtime.md) - WebSocket gateways and state deltas.
10. [Pinterest: Visual Search](./10-pinterest-visual-search.md) - Graph embeddings and ANN search.
11. [Notion: Sharding](./11-notion-sharding.md) - Moving from monolith to 480 shards.
12. [Shopify: Flash Sales](./12-shopify-flash-sales.md) - Edge-based queuing and thundering herd protection.
